About DORO

What is DORO?

DORO is a productivity-focused Pomodoro timer application designed to help you stay focused and make the most of your work sessions. Whether you're studying, working on a project, or tackling your to-do list, DORO helps you break your work into manageable intervals.

How It Works

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into focused intervals (traditionally 25 minutes), separated by short breaks. DORO makes it easy to implement this proven technique with a clean, intuitive interface.
